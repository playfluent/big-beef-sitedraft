Big Beef Records is a Dayton, Ohio-based record label that was originally founded in 1978 as a moniker for producing home taped recordings for Andy Valeri’s ‘living room band’ JA. Big Beef later went on to play a somewhat prominent role in the explosively creative Dayton music scene during the 1990’s and into the 21st century (before war and political turbulence directed it’s energy into other projects).

Here are some of the artists produced and/or promoted by Big Beef Records over the years…

  • The Mulchmen
  • Real Lulu
  • John Shough & Ultra Vega
  • Lab Partners
  • Morella’s Forest
  • The Pure Plastic Tree
  • The Lawn Jockeys (featuring Dr. Creep & The Organic Groove Continuum)
  • Gregg Spence

Other Artists affiliated with Big Beef through various forms of publishing/promotion/distribution;

  • cAge
  • Let’s Crash
  • The Convulsions
  • The Almighty Rogers

  • Mike Ruetschle
  • Backyard Astronauts
  • The Oxymorons
  • The Obvious
  • Admiral Ackbar’s Army
  • JA (the original Big Beef band)
